Profile:-

The Indirapuram Public School Girls has been established by Indirapuram Group of Institutions, Delhi NCR for Girl students. To Learn, To Serve, To Excel being our motto, we have a vision to attain the highest academic standards along with overall personality development of our students through a holistic approach. The school endeavours to provide quality education to its pupils based on a holistic approach where students will be motivated to explore and discover their innate talent and skills. It also aims to achieve levels of excellence in all fields thereby providing students with an analytical mind and a questioning outlook that gears them to become responsible citizens of society.
